Info

This is a music album website with visuals that react to the music in real time. Since I couldn’t use external libraries, I created an audio visualizer using TouchDesigner and exported it as a video. Essentially, it’s a video playlist website.

While listening to the music, I noticed a strong euphoric vibe. To match that feeling, I chose a purple color scheme and added liquid simulations for a dreamy effect. The overall design is minimal to let the visuals take center stage.
