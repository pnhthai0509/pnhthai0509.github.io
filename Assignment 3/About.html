Nguyen Hoang Thai (pnht) Phan is a multidisciplinary designer who passionate about videography, photography, and experimenting with various materials. He is passionate about applying his knowledge in designing and video producing to create visually captivating designs that effectively convey messages.

He takes his work very seriously and has a high sense of responsibility to create visually appealing images. With a great opportunity of working on more than 10 projects, he has been really enthusiastic about not only photographing but also videoing since high school. Throughout the years, putting his deep understanding into practice while capturing breathtaking scenery/memorable stories, he is able to organize the frames well to deliver meaningful messages to his audience.
