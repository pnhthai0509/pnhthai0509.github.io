DISSOLVING DIMENSIONS - MELBOURNE CBD

2024 - Video - Experimental - Student project


Dissolving Dimensions is a series that brings you to a digital world of vibrant cities via interactive and immersive experiences. Viewers can explore common spaces and feel the pulse of daily existence in every distinct area.


The “beating heart” of Melbourne, CBD, will serve as the starting point for this series. This melting pot of cultures is a constant source of fascination, a vibrant tapestry woven from countless threads of heritage and tradition. By using 3D scanning technology, the familiar reality is dissolved, which leads to the transformation into a mesmerizing point and the creation of a digital playground for everyone to interact with.

Role

3D Artist - Sound Designer
