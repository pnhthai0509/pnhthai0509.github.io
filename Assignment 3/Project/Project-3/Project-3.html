Efferescent

Video - Photography - Creative Project


I was grateful to be a member of management board for media department for Mass Media club. And this time, we decided to bring back our club tradition, Massgraphy, a creative project where media members can trully use our creativity. This project is called “Efferescent,” inspired by the classic tale of The Little Mermaid. But instead of singing about love and adventure, we're diving deep into the murky waters of pollution—because nothing says “fun” like raising awareness about ocean pollution while channeling our inner mermaids!


This project wasn’t just about engaging members and ensuring top-notch quality training and development. There was also a blood ritual involved—yes, you heard that right! It cost me my beloved Sony A7iv camera and left me with two scars on my head that are sure to be the highlight of any conversation. Who knew that saving the ocean could be so... bloody unforgettable?
