Streamer

2022 - Short film - Student project


Welcome to my first film!


This is my debut short film, aimed primarily at teenagers and their parents.


I chose to explore the world of streamers and gamers, a topic that sparks much debate in Vietnam, where it's often seen as a less-than-serious pursuit. But this film isn't just for gamers; it's for anyone who's chosen an entertaining or artistic career path.
